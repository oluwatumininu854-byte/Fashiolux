Our Simple Ordering Process

We keep the process transparent and focused on quality service for both retail and large-scale orders.

1. Inquire

Browse our catalog on the **Materials** page and use the **Contact** page to send a detailed inquiry with the specific fabric type and quantity you require.

2. Quote

We will respond within 24 hours with a comprehensive quote including pricing, available stock, and estimated delivery time to your location.

3. Confirm

Once you approve the quote, we will send an invoice for secure payment. Your order is then immediately prepared for shipping.
